Chaotic Cats is a 2-4 Player Multiplayer Party game my friends and I made under the name HeadBounceGames and released on steam.

Initially intended as a short game jam using Unity - we expanded our initial goals, creating 20 unique minigames over the course of a year and half.

We iterated on the game continuously, bringing it along to Smash Bros tournaments and other gaming events to get feedback.

Tasks for the game were split up into departments/teams (Art, Music, Programming) and we all worked together to bring the final proudct to release.

My particular role was as a programmer. I did contribute to some of the minigames, but my main contribution was creating the inter game transition screens, start/end animations, and start screen.
